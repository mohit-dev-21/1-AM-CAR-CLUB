VOLVO S90 Back to All Sedan

The Volvo S90 has it all: modern good looks, a smartly appointed interior, a plug-in-hybrid powertrain, and an 9.0-inch vertical infotainment screen that's as easy to use as a smartphone. The 2021 model received a subtle styling update to the front grille, front and rear bumper covers, and new taillights and wheel designs. A 316-hp turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder comes standard with an eight-speed automatic.

The plug-in-hybrid version has all-wheel drive and gets an EPA-estimated 60 MPGe combined. The EPA also says it's possible to go as far as 490 miles between fill ups with the PHEV. The interior features a gorgeous mix of high-quality materials such as brushed metal, leather, and open-pore wood. It also received a Top Safety Pick+ award from the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety, making it one of the safest cars to drive today.
